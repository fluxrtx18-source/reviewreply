Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

ReviewReply ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

ReviewReply processes all review text and AI-generated responses entirely on your device using Apple's Foundation Models framework. We do not:

  • Upload your review text to any server
  • Store your review text on any cloud infrastructure
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or analytics profiling
  • Access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or photos

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Review history: Review text you paste and the AI-generated responses. Stored locally only.
  • Tone configurations: Your custom tone names and instructions.
  • Usage counter: A date stamp tracking your daily free reply quota. No personal data is included.
  • Onboarding state: Whether you have completed onboarding (a boolean flag).

All local data can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

3. Subscriptions

ReviewReply Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Subscription management is handled by Apple's StoreKit framework.

4. App Extensions

The Share Extension and Keyboard Extension use an App Group to share your most recently copied responses between the main app and the extensions. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

5. Third-Party Services

ReviewReply does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. The app uses only Apple's first-party frameworks.

6. Children's Privacy

ReviewReply is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and in the app's App Store listing.
