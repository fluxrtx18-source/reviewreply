ReviewReply helps small business owners respond to customer reviews professionally — in seconds, not minutes.

Paste a 1-star review from Google, Yelp, App Store, or TripAdvisor. ReviewReply generates 3 professional response options in different tones — Apologetic, Professional, and Grateful — so you can pick the one that fits and post it immediately.

WHAT MAKES REVIEWREPLY DIFFERENT

- 100% On-Device AI: Powered by Apple's Foundation Models. Your reviews never leave your iPhone. No cloud. No data collection.

- 3 Tones, 1 Tap: Get three ready-to-post responses in Apologetic, Professional, and Grateful styles. Copy your favorite and paste it directly into Google Maps, Yelp, or App Store Connect.

- Fully Customizable Tones: Don't like the defaults? Create your own tones with custom AI instructions. Be Empathetic, Firm, Playful — whatever fits your brand.

- Review History: Every review and response is saved locally. Search, revisit, and reuse your best replies.

- Share Extension: Share a review directly from Google Maps or Yelp into ReviewReply for an instant professional response.

- Keyboard Extension: Access your saved responses from any app. Switch to ReviewReply Keys and tap to paste your reply.

WHO IT'S FOR

Small business owners, restaurant managers, salon owners, app developers, hotel managers — anyone who responds to online reviews and wants to sound professional under pressure.

PRICING

- Free: 1 professional reply per day
- ReviewReply Pro: Unlimited replies, all tones, full history — $9.99/month or $59.99/year

PRIVACY FIRST

ReviewReply uses Apple's on-device AI. Your review text is never uploaded, stored on servers, or shared with third parties. What happens on your iPhone stays on your iPhone.
